Welcome to My HTML-Only Project

Hi, and thanks for visiting my site! This is my first project using only HTML with no CSS, no JavaScript, just the basics. I wanted to see what I could build with the core language of the web and really understand how HTML works on its own.

I find it interesting how much you can do with just HTML. Even without any styling, you can organize content, add images, audio, and video, and make your site accessible to everyone. Working with only HTML has helped me focus on structure and meaning, instead of getting distracted by design details.
